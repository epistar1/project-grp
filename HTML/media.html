News

Trained to Grow Inc is mentioned in a news article about the expansion of an "intensive mentoring" program in Dane County. The article explains that county leaders have announced the expansion of the program in order to support at-risk children who are at risk of committing crimes. The goal of the program is to provide these children with mentoring support and to help prevent them from going down a criminal path. Trained to Grow Inc is mentioned as one of the organizations that will be involved in the expanded program, providing mentoring services to at-risk youth in the community.

Video

Felix Giboney IV is the founder and CEO of Trained to Grow Inc, a non-profit organization that is dedicated to supporting and empowering young people. In this interview, Felix discusses the mission and vision of Trained to Grow Inc, as well as the programs and services the organization offers to teens. He also shares some of the challenges and successes the organization has experienced, and talks about the impact that Trained to Grow Inc has had on the lives of young people in the community. This interview is a great opportunity to learn more about the work of Trained to Grow Inc, and to hear directly from Felix about the organization's goals and achievements.

Audio

Jerome Dillard is a journalist who has conducted an interview with Aaron Hicks of Nehemiah, James Morgan of the Madison Area Urban Ministries, and Felix Giboney from Trained to Grow. In the interview, the guests discuss the difficulties that African American youth often face, including issues related to family, education, and opportunities. They also share information about the programs and services their organizations offer to support and empower young people. Jerome Dillard asks the guests about their approaches to intervention and peer support, and how they strive to "walk with" young people to provide positive experiences and opportunities. This interview is a valuable resource for anyone who is interested in learning more about the challenges facing African American youth and the efforts of organizations to support and empower them.
