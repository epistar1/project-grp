About


Trained To Grow Inc. (TTG Inc.) is an organization established for the purpose of empowering and equipping Madison’s youth to become the leaders of their generation. TTG Inc.’s mission is to give youth the support, confidence and resources to excel in every area of life. TTG Inc. focuses on developing youth intellectually, emotionally, academically and mentally.

Get Involved


At our organization, we believe in the power of mentorship to transform the lives of teenagers. Our mentoring program pairs teens with experienced and caring mentors who provide guidance, support, and role modeling. Through one-on-one mentoring relationships, we aim to help teens develop the skills, confidence, and resilience they need to thrive. If you are a teenager interested in being matched with a mentor, or if you are an adult looking to make a positive impact in the life of a young person, please don't hesitate to get in touch.


Contact

We strive to support and empower teenagers in our community. If you are a teenager in need of assistance or if you are interested in getting involved with our cause, please don't hesitate to contact us. Our team is dedicated to making a positive difference in the lives of our youth.

Media


At TTG Inc., we are proud to share the stories and impact of our work through various forms of media. From articles and videos to podcasts and social media posts, we strive to keep our supporters informed and engaged with our mission. We also invite members of the media to learn more about our work and to share our stories with a wider audience. If you are a member of the media and would like to learn more about our organization, please don't hesitate to contact us. We would be happy to provide you with more information and to discuss potential collaboration opportunities.

Thank you to our sponsors!
