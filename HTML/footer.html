About

With everything we do, we want to challenge youth to be great. We want them to know and feel they are capable of greatness and beyond by changing the narrative of their lives to be authentic and their best self.

We serve the community of Madison, Wisconsin through encouraging, empowering and supporting the needs of challenge deprived (at risk) youth who lack an abundance of healthy support systems, guidance, and encouragement.
