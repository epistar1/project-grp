Everybody Eats


Introduces youth to Indoor Agriculture. The first pilot session will be a collaboration with an area elementary school that will introduce youth to the process of aquaponic and hydroponic growing of produce.

Teen Table Talks


Teen table talks are discussions or conversations that are specifically designed for teenagers. They often focus on topics that are relevant to young people, such as school, friendships, relationships, and challenges that they may face. Teen table talks can be led by adults, such as parents, teachers, or counselors, or they can be facilitated by the teenagers themselves.

Cooking for Culture


A monthly, one year program exploring the food that we and our neighbors consume. Each month will focus on a different culture, featuring professional chefs and cultural experts that will break down that culture's relationship with food
