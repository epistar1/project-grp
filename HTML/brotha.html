My Brotha

One on one mentoring where mentors will connect with their mentee in person at least once a week and will communicate with them daily via phone (call, text, etc.). The relationship will evolve, as the mentor will guide their mentee toward building strong self-awareness, higher self-esteem and improved academic achievement through a reflection process of analyzing the past, recognizing the present and preparing for the future with SMART goal setting, journaling and additional specialized activities.

The Barbershop


We are proud to offer a weekly interactive group conversation for teenagers. The focus of these conversations will be determined by the interests and concerns of the attending youth. The goal is to create a safe and supportive space for teens to discuss personal, social, and community issues that are relevant to their lives. By facilitating these discussions, we aim to empower youth to become active and engaged participants in their communities. We believe that by providing young people with the skills and support they need, they can become self-sufficient shareholders in the economic and social fabric of their communities. This, in turn, can help create a brighter future for both the individuals and the communities they call home.

Let's Rap


Let's Rap is a monthly session designed to engage and empower young people, their parents, and community members. These sessions will provide an opportunity for participants to come together and discuss important issues that are relevant to their lives. By fostering open and honest communication, we aim to promote positive growth, enrichment, and change through unity and collaboration. Each month, we will focus on a different series of topics, with the goal of providing participants with the tools and support they need to make a positive impact in their communities. We invite anyone who is interested in participating to join us for these engaging and informative discussions.
